COMPOSITION ABOUT MY SELF


My name is Tobi Ayomide Ariyibi, I am simple,gentle and confident fellow . My father is a farmer at Oyo state,he owns a cocoa plantation, who knows how to love his profession with passion and dedication. While my mother is a hardworking teacher at public school at her village in oyo whoose only passion is to transform the lives of her students with knowledge and a good vision.

I realized from the very start, tha the importance of passion, dedication,confidence and time management. We are two brothers among our parents and my grandmother. My family is what my parents had worked for,ie completely happy and satisfied one.

I am student of kwara state University of malete. studying LIS,currently at 200 Level.

Therefore, writing about myself, i am here to express myself and what i plan for my life is the total manifestation of what i do today. I try myself to be humble,passionate, dedicated,hardworking and honest.

I deem my parents as being my best mentors. I have learnt the real meaning and worth of life from my parents. i am with a happy,focused and hardworking family. That is definitely the greatest blessing of God for me.
